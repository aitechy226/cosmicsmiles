Insurance & Payment Options

We work with most major PPO plans to make your care as affordable as possible.

At CosmicSmiles Dental, we believe quality dental care should be accessible to every family. We accept most major PPO dental insurance plans and work hard to maximize your benefits before your first visit. Our team will verify your coverage, explain your out-of-pocket costs upfront, and help you make the most of your plan — no surprises at checkout.
