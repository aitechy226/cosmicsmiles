About Dr. Sudha

Caring for North Brunswick Families Since 2007

Dr. Jayasudha Samudrla — known warmly by her patients as "Dr. Sudha" — received her Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from the prestigious New York University College of Dentistry in 2007. Since founding CosmicSmiles Dental in North Brunswick, she has built a practice on one enduring principle: every patient deserves to be seen and treated by the same doctor, every time.

At CosmicSmiles, there are no associates, no rotating providers, and no starting your story over with a stranger. Dr. Sudha sees every patient personally — from routine cleanings to complex restorations and Invisalign cases. This continuity of care means she knows your history, your concerns, and your goals.

Born in India and having built her career in New Jersey, Dr. Sudha brings a deep understanding of the diverse communities she serves. She communicates fluently in English, Spanish, and Hindi, ensuring that language is never a barrier to excellent care. Her approach is gentle, unhurried, and focused entirely on your comfort.
